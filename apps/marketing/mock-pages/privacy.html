Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Aeonic Health Systems ("Aeonic," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our websites, the Aeonic Nexus platform, the Aeonic Continuum program, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). It is provided as a general template and should be reviewed by legal counsel before publication.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly — such as your name, contact details, practice information, and inquiry details — as well as information generated through use of the Services, including device and usage data. Where the Services are used to deliver care, protected health information ("PHI") may be processed by licensed providers and our affiliated clinical partners.

How we use information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Services;
  • To respond to inquiries and coordinate demos, onboarding, and support;
  • To enable clinical workflows performed by licensed providers;
  • To maintain security, prevent fraud, and meet legal and regulatory obligations.

Health information & HIPAA

Aeonic Nexus is designed to support HIPAA-compliant workflows. PHI is handled under applicable business associate and provider arrangements, with access controls, encryption, and audit logging. Clinical decisions and records are governed by the licensed providers delivering care.

How we share information

We share information with service providers, pharmacy and laboratory partners, and licensed clinicians as needed to deliver the Services, and as required by law. We do not sell personal information.

Data security & retention

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the information, and retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described or as required by law.

Your choices & rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete certain information, and to opt out of marketing communications. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to privacy@aeonichealthsystems.com.
