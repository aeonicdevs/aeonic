How the program works

Four steps from intake to a running protocol.

No clinic visits, no guesswork, nothing one-size-fits-all. Every step is guided by a licensed clinician and run from the patient's Aeonic app — and it doesn't end at the prescription.

1

Patient intake

A quick online intake captures the patient's history, goals, and numbers — about ten minutes, from anywhere.

2

Labs & physician review

We order the right bloodwork; a licensed clinician reviews the patient's results and builds a plan around their physiology.

3

Delivered to the patient

Each patient's personalized medication and supplement stack ships to their door, with a clear dose schedule and reminders.

4

Ongoing, adjusted care

Progress and labs are tracked and the protocol titrated over time — clinician and Aura, with the patient the whole way.
