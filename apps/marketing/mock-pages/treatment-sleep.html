A guided protocol — not a one-time script.

Every plan is personalized and clinician-monitored, and it moves through clear phases as the patient’s body responds. The clinician sets the cadence and adjusts it to them.

1

Assessment

Weeks 0–2

Sleep history, stress load, and labs — including cortisol rhythm — to find what's actually disrupting their sleep.

2

Foundation

Weeks 1–4

Lock in sleep hygiene plus magnesium, glycine, and L-theanine before adding anything prescriptive.

3

Active support

Weeks 4–10

Layer in circadian bioregulators or calming peptides chosen for their pattern, tracked against sleep and stress markers.

4

Maintenance

Ongoing

Move to an as-needed protocol, keeping the foundation in place for durable, drug-free sleep.
