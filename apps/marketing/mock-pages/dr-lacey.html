Dr. Lacey — portrait (2016-Chittle-8)
Dr. Lacey
Founder & Clinical Director · DPT

An intense passion + nutrition intuition + a whole lot of school = Lacey.

The science & authority

Two decades of clinical practice, codified into a system.

Aeonic is the work of Dr. Lacey — a clinician, athlete, and lifelong student who spent more than twenty years connecting exercise science, nutrition, and functional medicine into root-cause protocols. The whole system is the codified product of that practice: every protocol developed in real patient care, every clinical decision made by a licensed provider, and the platform built to run it all to standard.

Root-cause medicine, refined patient by patient over twenty years — then made repeatable, at scale.

Her path runs from a Doctor of Physical Therapy and exercise-biology training, through clinical internships at Johns Hopkins and Rancho Los Amigos National Rehabilitation Center, to full certification with the Institute for Functional Medicine. That foundation is what every Aeonic protocol — and the practices who license them — ultimately rest on.

Credentials & training

  • 2013–15Certified Functional Medicine Specialist — Institute for Functional Medicine (IFM)Full Applying Functional Medicine in Clinical Practice (AFMCP) certification plus advanced practice modules.
