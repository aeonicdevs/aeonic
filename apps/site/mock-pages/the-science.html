System Programs Platform

Root cause, not symptoms.

Most care treats the number on the chart. We treat the system underneath it — precision medicine that uses each patient’s biomarkers to find what’s actually driving how they feel, then applies protocols refined over 20 years of clinical practice.

Symptoms are downstream. We treat the system that produces them.

A lab value out of range, low energy, stalled weight, poor sleep — these are outputs. Conventional care suppresses the output; the driver underneath keeps compounding. Systems-based medicine asks what’s producing it, corrects the inputs, and lets the output normalize on its own.

Conventional

Treat the number

A symptom appears, a medication masks it. The underlying driver — metabolic, hormonal, inflammatory — keeps compounding underneath.

Aeonic

Treat the cause

We map the biomarkers, isolate the actual driver, and correct it — so the improvement is structural, not cosmetic, and it holds.

The difference

Measured, not guessed

Every decision is anchored to data — labs in, protocol out, re-tested over time. Nothing is one-size-fits-all.

A closed loop, run continuously.

The same loop runs behind every protocol — the engine that turns a patient’s biology into a plan, then keeps tuning it to the response.

01 · Test

Establish the baseline

Comprehensive panels capture where the patient actually is — across metabolism, hormones, inflammation, and more.

02 · Interpret

Read against optimal

Markers are read against optimal ranges, not just “normal” — and against each other, to find the driver.

03 · Protocol

Build the plan

Therapies, dosing, titration, and supplement support are selected to the biology and the goal, screened for interactions.

04 · Deliver

Run it, monitored

The protocol runs in the app with reminders, check-ins, and a clinician one tap away — between visits, not just at them.

05 · Re-test

See what moved

Repeat panels show what actually changed — the proof the protocol is working, in the data.

06 · Adjust

Tune to the response

The protocol evolves to the result — the loop closes and runs again. Optimization compounds over time.

Therapies — and what they actually do.

Not a grab-bag of trends. A focused set of evidence-based therapeutic classes, each chosen for a mechanism.

GLP-1 & metabolic

Rewire appetite & glucose

GLP-1 receptor agonists mimic a gut hormone that slows gastric emptying and signals fullness — reducing intake while improving insulin sensitivity and glucose control.

Peptides

Signal repair & recovery

Targeted peptides act on the body’s own repair and signaling pathways — tissue healing, recovery, growth-hormone support, and immune modulation.

Hormone optimization

Restore the baseline

Lab-guided hormone therapy returns sex hormones, thyroid, and the endocrine axis to optimal — the foundation under energy, strength, and drive.

NAD+ & cellular

Fuel the mitochondria

NAD+, methylation, and mitochondrial support restore cellular energy production — the longevity layer beneath stamina and resilience.
