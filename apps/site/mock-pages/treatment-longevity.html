A guided protocol — not a one-time script.

Every plan is personalized and clinician-monitored, and it moves through clear phases as the patient’s body responds. The clinician sets the cadence and adjusts it to them.

1

Longevity panel

Weeks 0–2

A deep biomarker panel — metabolic, inflammatory, hormonal — sets their starting point and surfaces what to prioritize first.

2

Foundation

Months 1–2

Establish the daily base: sleep, training, and a foundational supplement stack — NMN, resveratrol, omega-3, CoQ10 — that everything else builds on.

3

Targeted therapies

Months 2–6

Layer in precision therapies — NAD+, pulsed rapamycin, or bioregulator peptides — chosen for their biomarkers and tracked against them.

4

Re-test & refine

Every 6 months

Repeat the panel, see what moved, and refine. The protocol evolves as their biomarkers — and the science — do.
