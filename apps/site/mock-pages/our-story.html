Our story

It began as one clinician’s practice — not a product.

Aeonic didn’t start as a company. It started as twenty years of real patient care — a clinician chasing root causes, refining protocols person by person, long before any of it had a name. Everything you see today is that practice, codified.

Where it started

Two decades of root-cause medicine, refined one patient at a time.

Dr. Lacey spent more than twenty years connecting exercise science, nutrition, and functional medicine into protocols that actually moved how people felt — hormones, metabolism, recovery, sleep, cognition. Not symptoms in isolation, but the whole interconnected system.

Over time a pattern became impossible to ignore: the protocols worked, but they lived in one clinician’s head and one clinic’s walls. To reach more people without losing the standard, the work had to be written down — dosing, titration, biomarker targets, screening — and built into something that could be run to standard, every time.

Dr. Lacey — clinic
The path

From a practice, to a protocol library, to a system.

2000–’09

The practice

Two decades of hands-on clinical work — exercise biology, a Doctor of Physical Therapy, neurology internships at Johns Hopkins and Rancho Los Amigos — laid the foundation.

2013–’15

The method

Full certification with the Institute for Functional Medicine turned intuition into a repeatable, root-cause framework across hormones, metabolism, gut, immune and cognition.

The turn

Codifying the work

Every protocol — dosing, titration, biomarker targets, contraindication screening — was written down as a clinical standard that could outlive any single appointment.

The platform

The system, in software

Intake, protocols, prescribing, pharmacy, labs, billing and Aura — one HIPAA-compliant console to run the whole program to standard.

Aeonic Health

Care, directly

We put the system to work ourselves — a direct-to-member longevity brand built entirely on the platform.

Aeonic Connect

Open to practices

The same system, white-labeled for clinics — so any practice can run precision longevity medicine to the same standard, in weeks.

What we believe

The principles the system is built on.

01

The body is a system

Hormones, metabolism, recovery and cognition are one interconnected system. We read the biomarkers and adjust the inputs together — never symptoms in isolation.

02

Provider-led, always

Software accelerates clinicians; it never replaces them. Every protocol and prescription is reviewed and signed by a licensed provider.

03

Standard before scale

The protocol is the product. We codify it so it holds whether we’re treating a patient directly or backing a partner clinic across the country.
